Rustikalna elegancija uz modernu udobnost.

Rustic elegance with modern comfort.

Okružena zelenilom i tišinom, ova kuća je idealna za obitelji, parove i putnike koji traže opuštajući boravak u prirodnom okruženju. Objekt spaja tradicionalni etno stil uređenja s modernom tehnologijom, stvarajući jedinstvenu i ugodnu atmosferu. Kuća je prikladna za parove, obitelji, prijatelje i poslovne goste koji žele mir, privatnost i dobru povezanost s Kutinskim područjem.

The space is designed with warm wooden details and a homelike atmosphere, while offering everything needed for a comfortable stay. The house is suitable for couples, families, friends, and business guests looking for peace, privacy, and convenient access to the Kutina area.

LokacijaLocation

Holiday House Zoki nalazi se u mirnom prirodnom okruženju u blizini Kutine, idealnom za goste koji žele odmor, privatnost i boravak okružen zelenilom.

Holiday House Zoki is set in peaceful natural surroundings near Kutina, ideal for guests looking for relaxation, privacy, and a stay surrounded by greenery.

Prirodno okruženje za miran boravak

Natural surroundings for a peaceful stay

Smještaj je odličan izbor za one koji žele pobjeći od gradske gužve i uživati u tišini, zelenilu i opuštenoj atmosferi. Okolica nudi šumske puteve, livade i autentičan moslavački krajolik, a Kutina je i dalje lako dostupna.

The house is a great choice for guests who want to step away from city crowds and enjoy quiet, greenery, and a relaxed atmosphere. The surrounding area offers forest roads, meadows, and an authentic Moslavina landscape, while Kutina remains easily accessible.
